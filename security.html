Security Policy

Your security is our top priority. Learn how we protect your payment information.

At YelaCode, we take the security of your payment information seriously. All payment card details are transmitted securely using industry-standard SSL encryption, ensuring your data is protected during every transaction.

We do not store your card details on our servers. Our payment partners are PCI DSS compliant, adhering to the highest standards of data security in the industry. This means your sensitive information is handled with the utmost care and protection.

If you have any questions or concerns about our security practices, please contact us. Your trust and peace of mind are important to us, and we are committed to maintaining the highest level of security for all our customers.
