Transaction Currency

All transactions on YelaCode are processed in Malaysian Ringgit (MYR).

YelaCode operates in Malaysia and, as such, all prices and transactions are denominated in Malaysian Ringgit (MYR). This ensures transparency and consistency for our local customers, allowing for straightforward billing and payment processing.

For international customers, your payment provider or bank may automatically convert the transaction amount to your local currency based on prevailing exchange rates. Please note that additional fees or charges may apply depending on your financial institution's policies.

If you have any questions about currency conversion or need a quote in another currency, please contact our customer service team. We are committed to making your experience as smooth and clear as possible.
